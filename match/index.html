The project

This fashion project was inspired by the whimsical movies directed by Hayao Miyazaki. It was developed in 2013 as part of the FAAP Moda contest at Fundação Armando Álvares Penteado, SP. All patterns and illustrations were created by me, and photography was done by Flavio Luiz Matangrano.
